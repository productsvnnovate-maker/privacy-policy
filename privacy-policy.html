Community Management System

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 24, 2025

Last Updated: April 24, 2025

Patanvada Mochi Samaj ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of its community members. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard information within the Patanvada Mochi Samaj – Community Directory & Family Tree App. By using this application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Section 01

Information We Collect

To provide you with a secure and accurate community directory experience, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Identification: Full name, mobile number, date of birth, gender, and photograph.
  • Family Information: Spouse, children, parents, and extended family details added by the member to the family profile.
  • Contact Details: Address, city, state, and email address (if provided).
  • Occupational Details: Profession or business information shared optionally by members.
  • App Usage Data: Login timestamps, session activity, and feature interactions for security and improvement purposes.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support purposes.

All profile information is submitted voluntarily by members or their authorized family representatives. No information is collected without the member's knowledge.

Section 02

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for community-related purposes within the Patanvada Mochi Samaj network:

  • To create and maintain your community member profile in the directory.
  • To enable the interactive Family Tree feature, showing generational relationships.
  • To allow other verified community members to find and connect with you.
  • To send important announcements, community news, and notifications within the app.
  • To verify member identity and maintain the integrity of the directory.
  • To support administrators in managing and moderating community data.
  • To improve app performance, fix issues, and enhance user experience.

We do not use your data for commercial advertising, sell your information to third parties, or share it outside the community network.

Section 03

Data Access & Visibility

Your privacy is our priority. Access to member data is strictly limited:

  • Verified Members Only: Profile information is visible only to approved and logged-in members of the Patanvada Mochi Samaj community.
  • Administrators: Authorized community administrators can access and manage profiles to ensure accuracy and resolve disputes.
  • Approval Before Visibility: All new profiles go through an administrator verification process before appearing in the directory.
  • No Public Access: The app and its data are not accessible to the general public or unregistered users.

The App supports Bilingual access (English and Gujarati), but language preference does not affect data privacy settings.

Section 04

Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your personal and family information seriously. The following measures are in place:

  • Data is stored on secure servers with access control and encryption protocols.
  • Mobile number–based secure login ensures only authorized users access accounts.
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring are conducted to detect unauthorized access.
  • All data transmissions between the app and server are protected using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

While we implement strong safeguards, no digital system is entirely immune to risk. We encourage members to keep their login credentials confidential and report any suspicious activity immediately.

Section 05

Your Rights & Choices

As a member of the Patanvada Mochi Samaj app, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View all personal and family information stored in your profile at any time.
  • Update: Modify or correct your personal details and family information through the app.
  • Deletion: Request the removal of your profile and associated data by contacting the community administrator.
  • Objection: Raise concerns about how your data is being used or who has access to it.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact the community administrator using the details provided at the end of this policy.

Section 06

Children's Privacy

The Patanvada Mochi Samaj App is intended for use by adult community members. Information about minors (children under 18) may be included as part of a family profile, entered by a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly allow minors to register independently or create accounts.

If you believe a minor's information has been entered without appropriate consent, please contact the administrator immediately for review and action.

Section 07

Third-Party Services

The App may use limited third-party services (such as hosting infrastructure or push notification services) to deliver core functionality. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use community data for their own purposes.

We do not integrate with social media platforms, advertising networks, or any external data brokers. No community data is shared beyond what is strictly necessary to operate the App.

Section 08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. When significant changes are made, we will notify members via in-app notification or announcement.

The date of the most recent revision will always appear at the top of this page. We encourage members to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect their information.
